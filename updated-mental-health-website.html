Helpful Mental Health Resources

Your journey to better mental health starts here. We've curated a list of reliable organizations in the Netherlands that offer support and assistance for various mental health issues.

Quick Access

  • Emergency: If you're in immediate danger, call 112
  • Suicide Prevention Hotline: 0800-0113 (free) or 113
  • General Mental Health Support: +31 (0)33 303 2400 (MIND)

GGZ Nederland

GGZ Nederland is the leading association for mental health and addiction care in the Netherlands. They represent a wide network of mental health care providers and work towards improving the quality and accessibility of mental health services across the country.

Services offered:

  • Coordination of mental health policies and practices
  • Advocacy for mental health awareness and destigmatization
  • Information hub for patients, professionals, and policymakers
  • Collaboration with international mental health organizations

Phone: +31 (0)33 460 89 00

Visit Website

113 Suicide Prevention

113 Suicide Prevention is a national organization dedicated to preventing suicide through direct support, research, and education. They offer a range of services aimed at helping individuals in crisis and those concerned about loved ones.

Services offered:

  • 24/7 crisis helpline for immediate support
  • Online chat service for those who prefer text-based communication
  • Self-help modules and resources for managing suicidal thoughts
  • Training programs for professionals and gatekeepers

Phone: 0800-0113 (free) or 113

Visit Website

MIND

MIND is a prominent mental health advocacy organization in the Netherlands. They focus on improving the lives of people with mental health issues through support, information sharing, and campaigning for better mental health policies.

Services offered:

  • Peer support groups and networks
  • Mental health information and educational resources
  • Advocacy for mental health rights and improved care
  • Campaigns to reduce stigma and raise awareness

Phone: +31 (0)33 303 2400

Visit Website

Depression Association

The Depression Association (Depressie Vereniging) is a specialized organization focusing on supporting individuals experiencing depression and their loved ones. They provide a platform for sharing experiences, gaining knowledge, and finding support.

Services offered:

  • Support groups for people with depression and their families
  • Information about depression, its symptoms, and treatment options
  • Advocacy for better depression care and research
  • Online forum for peer support and experience sharing

Phone: +31 (0)30 254 3054

Visit Website

FAQ

  1. How do I know if I need professional help?

    If your mental health is affecting your daily life, relationships, or work, it may be time to seek professional help.

  2. Are these services confidential?

    Yes, all listed organizations adhere to strict confidentiality policies.

  3. What if I can't afford mental health care?

    Many organizations offer sliding scale fees or free services. Contact them directly to discuss your options.
